**Extra, extra, read all about it.**

Bunny shot a hole-in-one on the third hole at [Cape Kidnappers](http://www.capekidnappers.com).

**A little backstory.**

What we had not yet told you is that the day before, on the practice range at [Wairakei Golf Club](/blog/2015-02-02-dateline-january-28-2015-golf-at-wairakei-golf-and-sanctuary), Bunny had set her clubs down on the grass to warm up. When she picked them up there was duck poop on them, a fair bit on her arm, and a touch on her shirt. We all told her, with the appropriate gravity, that bird poop is good luck.

So now for the rest of the story.

**A day of threes.**

![The third hole](https://res.cloudinary.com/dsru5pryd/image/upload/f_auto,q_auto/images/2015/cape-kidnappers-third-hole.jpg)

- The 3rd hole
- Playing a Callaway ball with a 3 on it
- The 30th day of the month
- The 3rd day in a row that we played golf

**Her first hole-in-one.**

![Ace Bunny](https://res.cloudinary.com/dsru5pryd/image/upload/f_auto,q_auto/images/2015/bunny-celebration-1.jpg)

**Made official.**

A few days later, Cape Kidnappers sent us a photo of the plaque, with Bunny's name engraved on it. Hers was the second hole-in-one at the course in 2015. The first one belonged to a PGA pro.

![Bunny's name on the plaque at Cape Kidnappers](https://res.cloudinary.com/dsru5pryd/image/upload/f_auto,q_auto/images/2015/bunny-plaque.jpg)

Congratulations, Ace.
